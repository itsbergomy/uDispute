💡 How to Use the Dual-Letter Strategy

  • • Send both letters on the same day via certified mail with return receipt requested.
  • • The CRA letter triggers the 30-day FCRA investigation clock and preserves your right to sue.
  • • The furnisher letter bypasses e-OSCAR — the creditor reads your ACTUAL dispute, not a 2-digit code.
  • • Under Regulation V (§ 1022.43(e)), the furnisher must report results to ALL bureaus.
  • • Keep copies + certified mail receipts — evidence for CFPB complaints or lawsuits.
