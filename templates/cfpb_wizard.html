{# ═══ STEP 5 — What Happens Next ═══ #}

Step 5 — What Happens Next

⏱ Company Response Timeline

The company has 15 days to respond to the CFPB. Most respond within 7 days to avoid regulatory action. You'll receive an email from the CFPB with their response.

🔄 If Verified / Not Resolved

If the account comes back verified, repeat this process. Each CFPB complaint creates a federal record. You can also:

  • File another CFPB complaint (different narrative)
  • Send a Bureau Assault dispute letter
  • Escalate to arbitration demand
  • File with your State Attorney General
📋 Save Your Complaint Number

The CFPB will assign a complaint number after submission. Save it — you'll need it to track the response and reference in any follow-up complaints or dispute letters.
