How It Works

Four steps. Full lifecycle.

1

Upload & Parse

Upload your credit report PDF. Vision AI extracts every negative account and identifies specific inaccuracies — wrong balances, misreported dates, incorrect payment history, status contradictions.

2

Strategize

Choose your weapon — 4 legal prompt packs (Default, Consumer Law, ACDV Enforcement, Arbitration), dual-letter strategy to bypass e-OSCAR, or a Notice of Dispute to all 3 bureaus simultaneously.

3

Generate

AI writes letters that address your specific errors — citing Metro 2 field numbers, FCRA statutes, and federal case law. Not templates. Case-specific letters built from your parsed data.

4

Dispute & Escalate

Mail via USPS, track delivery, log bureau responses, and escalate automatically. The AI recommends your next move based on what came back — Consumer Law, Arbitration, or CFPB complaint.
